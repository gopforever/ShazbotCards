Privacy Policy for ShazbotCards

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your eBay account to ShazbotCards, we access:

  • Your eBay listing information (titles, prices, views, etc.)
  • Your eBay account username
  • Analytics data about your listings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your eBay data to:

  • Display your listings in the ShazbotCards dashboard
  • Provide AI-powered title optimization suggestions
  • Predict listing performance
  • Sync changes between ShazbotCards and eBay

3. Data Storage

Your eBay authorization tokens are stored locally in your browser only. We do not store your eBay credentials or tokens on any server.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • eBay API: To access your listing data
  • GitHub Models API: For AI-powered features (title optimization, performance prediction)

5. Data Security

All communication with eBay uses secure HTTPS connections. Your authentication tokens are encrypted and never shared with third parties.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Disconnect your eBay account at any time
  • Revoke ShazbotCards' access from your eBay account settings
  • Delete all locally stored data by clearing your browser's local storage

7. Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, please contact us via GitHub: github.com/gopforever/ShazbotCards
